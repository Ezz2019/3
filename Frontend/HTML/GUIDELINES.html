Auctions

  • An online auction allows interested buyers to bid against each other for a particular item. At the end of the auction, the person with the highest bid receives the item.
  • A benefit of an online auction is the removal of the physical limitations of a traditional auction

To win in online auctions:

  • Understand the Auction Rules
  • Know the Bid Clock
  • Don't Start with your maximum bid
  • Monitor the Bid Increments
